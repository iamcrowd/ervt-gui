NuSMV-BDD/NuSMV-BMC

NuSMV is a reimplementation and extension of SMV, the first model checker based on BDDs. NuSMV has been designed to be an open architecture for model checking, which can be reliably used for the verification of industrial designs, as a core for custom verification tools, as a testbed for formal verification techniques, and applied to other research areas.

Aalta

Aalta(Another Algorithm for LTL To Büchi Automata) tool originally is developed as an LTL-to-Büchi translator. Compared with the traditional tableau-based translation, such as SPOT, it is implemented with a new algorithm, which introduces the concept of Obligation Set for LTL formulas. More recently, a novel on-the-fly LTL satisfiability checking algorithm is also proposed based on the Obligation Set. So far, Aalta integrates two functions: LTL-to-Büchi translation and LTL satisfiability checking.
